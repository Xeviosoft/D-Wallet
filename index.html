    Welcome to DWallet

    DWallet is a frictionless decentralized mobile wallet for iOS and Android for buying, sending and receiving Doge Coin! The purpose od DWallet is to be accessable, easy and fun to use! Furthermore, we strive to get Doge into the hands of the people!
