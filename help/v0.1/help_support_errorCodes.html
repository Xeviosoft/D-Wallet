MultiDoge shows me a specific error/exception. What does it mean ?

When MultiDoge encounters a problem you will often see an error message. This can be in a small dialog box or written in the status line at the bottom of MultiDoge. Messages written to the status line are also repeated in the ' Messages' tab.

Like clues in a detective story you can often work out what the original problem was from these error messages. See if your error message / exception is listed below - if it is click on the link for more information.

  • BlockStoreException

  • Could not decrypt bytes

  • Insufficient funds
